ABOUT US

Direct Dispatch is using state of the art technologies to innovate aggressivley for auto transportation industy

Located in Boca Raton, Florida, Direct Dispatch is an online business that has been serving the U.S. transportation industry for years. Our online platform offers a user-friendly means for customers to search for and book a reliable motor transportation service provider online.

With a strong commitment to delivering the highest quality services to clients, Direct Dispatch connects shipping customers to an established motor carrier, without the use of a shipping broker. You can use our web-based secure portal to connect with a leading motor carrier anywhere, anytime!

Our mission is to help make vehicle transportation easier, faster and more convenient; hence we give our customers the services they deserve! Whether you want to want to transport your motorcycle, car even heavy equipment, our online portal grants you access to over 15,000 licensed motor carriers in the United States under one roof. And most importantly, the elimination of middlemen means you don’t have to pay exorbitantly priced broker fees.

Not only do you get instant shipping pricing quotes from various motor carriers, but we offer immediate alerts for you to track your vehicle. Our web-based system is highly secure. You can be assured that your private contact information will never be shared with an external party.

Having several years of experience under our belt, we have made the auto shipping process more efficient and hassle-free. Our advanced online software ensures that customers get security and complete peace of mind for moving their vehicles across the U.S.
