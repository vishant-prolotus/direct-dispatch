HOW IT WORKS

Direct Dispatch connects you directly with a carrier without the need for a broker or their fee.

Request Price

Utilizing Direct Dispatch's proprietary software to input a few basic parameters you will be presented instantly with the final cost for your transport order. 

Confirm Order

Upon placing your ship request, an order confirmation will be issued confirming your order is being processed by one of Direct Dispatch's logistics coordinators. 

Carrier Confirmation

Direct Dispatch takes the time to approve every driver within their network assuring you that communication during every step of the way will be timely and clear.

Transport Delivered

Relax while your automobile is shipped safely, reliably and affordably to it's final destination by a qualified Direct Dispatch Carrier. 

Our simple to use interface walks you through each step from start to finish.
