BACKGROUND

 

A.                Direct Dispatch operates a website accessible at https://direct-dispatchers.com/ (hereinafter the Website) through which persons or entities (hereinafter Users) may attempt to locate an authorized motor carrier to transport vehicles or other cargo from a specified origin to a designated destination;

 

B.                 Subscribing Carrier is an interstate or intrastate motor carrier operating pursuant to authority granted to it to do so by the FMCSA and the U.S. Dept. of Transportation (DOT) under the license(s) listed above and/or pursuant to other permits and/or licenses to operate as an intrastate motor carrier granted to Carrier by one or more state regulatory agencies;

 

C.                Subscribing Carrier desires to be able to post rates on the Website for specified routes or traffic lanes for which it is willing to transport vehicles or other cargo from origins to destinations and/or to otherwise present estimates or quotes to Users of the Website for the transportation of vehicles or other cargo from a Users specified origin to the Users designated destination;

 

D.                Direct Dispatch and Subscribing Carrier desire to enter into this Carrier Subscription Agreement to set forth and memorialize the terms of the agreement between them so that Subscribing Carrier may post rates and/or extend quotes or estimates to Users through use of the Website.
